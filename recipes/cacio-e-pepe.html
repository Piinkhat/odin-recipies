Cacio e pepe.

Description:

Cacio e Pepe is a quick and simple pasta dish that contains only a few ingredients!

ingredients:

  • 6 oz. pasta
  • kosher salt
  • butter
  • ground black pepper
  • finely grated Pecorino Romano cheese

Steps:

  1. boil 3 quarts of water season with kosher salt
  2. add pasta to boiling water, stir ocassionally
  3. remove pasta 2 minutes before tender, drain pasta and sace 3/4 cup of pasta water.
  4. melt butter in skillet over medium heat
  5. add black pepper and toast
  6. add cooked pasta to skillet
  7. add pasta water to skillet and simmer
  8. turn heat to low and add grated Pecorino Romano
  9. stir and toss until cheese sauce coats pasta
  10. serve!
